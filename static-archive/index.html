Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Inquiries

Clear, honest, and direct answers to help you feel confident before your first consultation.

What should I expect during my first aesthetic consultation?

+

Your journey begins with an in-depth 45-minute clinical assessment. We utilize multi-spectral skin analysis to diagnose sub-surface vascularity, melanin, and hydration levels, and combine it with a review of your hormonal profile if you are experiencing hair or metabolic weight concerns. From this, Dr. Anya designs a bespoke therapy roadmap.

Are the laser and microneedling treatments painful?

+

Patient comfort is our top priority. We apply premium, medical-grade topical numbing anesthetics 30 minutes before any fractional laser or microneedling treatments. The sensation is described as a mild, warm tingling or light scratching. There is absolutely no intense pain, and we incorporate cool cryo-air soothing during the process.

How long will it take before I see visible results?

+

Clinical skincare treatments like chemical peels and HydraFacials yield immediate, glowing results within 24 hours. Structural treatments like exosome microneedling, laser resurfacing, or hair restoration cycles require collagen synthesis and follicle activation time—visible improvements typically emerge within 4 to 8 weeks, peaking at 3 months.

Do you offer customized programs for both men and women?

+

Absolutely. Awish Clinic is built with a deep understanding that male and female skin, hormone systems, and aesthetic goals differ fundamentally. Our treatments, diagnostic protocols, and medical pathways are tailored explicitly to gender-specific physiology to ensure maximum safety and biological efficacy.
