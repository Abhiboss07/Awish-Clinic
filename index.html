05 / Action Plan
🗺️ 90-Day Roadmap to First $5K
1
Week 1–2: Learn the Stack
Get comfortable with Cursor + Claude + Next.js + Supabase. Build 2–3 practice projects. No need to be perfect.
2
Week 3–4: Find Your Niche
Research pain points in Reddit, Indie Hackers, Twitter. Find a problem people pay to solve. Validate before building.
3
Month 2: Build & Launch MVP
Ship a minimal but working product. Use AI to code 80% of it. Launch on Product Hunt, HN, Reddit, Twitter.
4
Month 3: Get First 10 Paying Users
Do things that don't scale. DM people directly. Offer free trials. Collect feedback. Iterate fast. Hit $1K MRR.
5
Month 4–6: Scale to $5K+
Add SEO, content marketing, referral program. Launch adjacent products. Build audience. Expand pricing tiers.
6
Month 6–12: Hit $20K
Stack multiple revenue streams. Add freelance/agency while SaaS grows. Or double down on what's working.
⚠️ Reality Check $20K/month is absolutely achievable with vibe coding — but it requires consistent execution, not just building. Most people who fail quit too early or skip validation. The pattern that works: validate an idea → ship fast → talk to users → iterate → monetize. AI tools give you a 10x speed advantage, but business fundamentals still apply.
